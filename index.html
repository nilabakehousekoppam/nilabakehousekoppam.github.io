About Nila Bake House

Nila Bake House is a cozy home-town bakery started with a simple idea – to bring fresh, homemade taste to every celebration.

From birthday cakes and anniversary specials to festival treats and tea-time snacks, every item is baked in small batches using quality ingredients and a lot of love.

We also take custom orders for theme cakes, kids' birthday cakes, and bulk snack orders for offices, schools, and family functions.
